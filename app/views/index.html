Changes in this version

This is the first version of the Book official visits prototype. This service is a modification of the Appointments and Activities service. This first version contains the end-to-end appintments journey with additional pages for book an official visit.

Changes include:

  • updates to the 'create an appointment' journey
  • updates to the 'view an appointment' journeys
  • addition of 'edit an appointment'

Supporting documents

  • High-level journey diagram (miro)
  • Mapped out user journeys (miro)

1. updates to the 'create an appointment' journey

We have made modifications and additions to the 'create an appointment' journey in order to accomodate the selection of official visits as an appointment type, and the routing of the users through additional pages to select official and social contacts. At present, we are unclear which pages we might be able to skip past in this scenario (tiers etc).

  • appointment name (type)
  • add an official contact
  • official contact list
  • add a social contact
  • social contact list
  • check your answers

2. updates to the 'view an appointment' journeys

To find official vists appointments, we priopose adding 'official visit' to the filter options on the appointments dashboard. In order to accomodate official visits, we have modified the appointment summary to accomodate the additional information created when creating an official visit appointment.

  • appointment dashboard
  • appointment summary
